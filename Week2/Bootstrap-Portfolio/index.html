About Me

The state of legal marketing? Evolving rapidly. That's because technology in all its forms - marketing technology, Legal Tech, Big Data, and more - is disrupting the profession, from the way lawyers provide services to clients to the ways in which they market their practices, analyze their results, and understand their opportunities.

After three decades inside and outside of law firms around the world providing a broad range of marketing, communications, and business development solutions, I've decided to jump headfirst into the (r)evolution of legal tech with a certificate in coding from Case Western Reserve University's web development bootcamp.

I’ve lived in the United States, France, and Hong Kong. Currently I'm in Cleveland Heights, Ohio, with my wife and two children. I'm active in the CHUH schoool district community, where I'm president of the Roxboro Middle School PTA, cofounder of the Heights Performing Arts Camp, and on the Board of Directors of Reaching Heights, a community organization dedicated to providing support to our local public schools and students. When I’m not out on my bike or in the yoga studio, you’ll probably find me reading.
